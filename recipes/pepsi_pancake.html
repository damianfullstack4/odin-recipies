Pepsi Pancake

Description

Classic pancakes made with the world-renowned soft drink Pepsi-cola!

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups of flour
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1½ teaspoons of baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 1½ cups of milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 3 tablespoons of pepsi

Steps

  1. Heat and grease your skillet
  2. Add dry ingredients into a bowl and whisk them
  3. Add wet ingredients into a bowl and whisk them
  4. Pour wet ingredients onto the dry ingredients and whisk them.
  5. Let batter sit for 5 minutes
  6. Pour ⅓ of batter onto the skillet
  7. Wait until there are bubbles and then flip
  8. Cook for 2-3 minutes on the other side
  9. Remove pancake
