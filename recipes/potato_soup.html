Baked Potato Soup

Home

Description

A thick, creamy, and delicious soup that can easily be scaled in size to feed the whole family and still have leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 12 slices of bacon
  • ⅔ cup of butter
  • ⅔ cup of all-purpose flour
  • 7 cups of milks
  • 4 large baked potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1¼ cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place bacon in a large skillet on medium-high heat until evenly browned ~ 8-10 minutes
  2. Drain bacon slices onto a paper towel and crumble before setting aside
  3. Melt butter in a stockpot over medium heat
  4. Gradually add flour and whisk until well mixed
  5. Slowly pour milk in while constantly whisking until smooth and thickened
  6. Add potatoes and onions
  7. Bring to a boil while stirring frequently
  8. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes
  9. Stir in bacon, cheddar cheese, sour cream, salt, and pepper
  10. Continue cooking and stirring until cheese is melted
  11. Enjoy!
